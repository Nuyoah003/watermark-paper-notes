| **Year** | 2024 |

## Quick Summary

Proposes **In-Context Watermarking (ICW)** — a prompt-engineering-based approach that embeds watermarks into LLM-generated text **without accessing model weights, logits, or the sampling process**. Four strategies are proposed at different granularities: Unicode, Initials, Lexical, and Acrostics.
